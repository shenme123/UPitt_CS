Social network forms a network that projects into real world. It relies on people who are using it, at the same time the users are relying on it, too.



Positive



As a kind of new media or internet community, since social networks are accepted by almost all the people, there must be many advantages.

Microscopically, or personally, people manage their relationships more easily, enhance the relationships actually. One can easily get in touch with whom they want, get to know about their life style, their pictures and mood. So for a boy, before chasing a girl, to get her Facebook nickname is a good way to know things about her. Moreover, there will be many sharing information including living experiences, amusing videos, beautiful pictures, etc. So surfing on social networks is a good way to get useful information and kill time.

Macroscopically, social networks play an important role in information spreading. Because surfing in social network is more interesting than watching news or something like that, people always spend more time on it. And at the same time, there are more and more people specified in various fields using social networks, then there would be plenty of information in which there should be some attract the user. So by killing time, people gain the information they want with no intention for that. By this way, information will spread faster, which will contribute to a flatter world. Good things in society are easy to be known to be spoken highly of and the virtue will be passed on, and infamies are easy to be known to be spitted on by public. Thus, social networks can help contribute to a better society.

Negative



On the contrary, social networks also have negative impacts to the society.

Microscopically, people will remain less privacy by the impact of social networks. Users are requested better to fill in the real personal information when signing up, as a result of which makes them easy to be reached. Most people don¡¯t have enough personal protection awareness, thinking they don¡¯t have much useful information as an ordinary being. But sometimes this could really harm their normal life and make troubles. Besides, as a service provider, social networks company should also enhance their security to provide better information protection for their users.

Macroscopically, via social networks, the speed of information spreading is too fast. That is not a good thing under certain circumstance. For example, government shall stabilize the mood of people when there is a terrorist incident, and handle the incident with people not aware of. But if the terrorist publish this on a Facebook page, it will immediately cause panic, and the panic will quickly spread to the whole society. This example is a little ridiculous, but sometimes information is not the more quickly it spreads, the better.
