New creations come out with new technologies. Undoubtedly, computer leads people into a digital world, internet makes the world flat. Email is the first creation that allows people to contact with others online. From the day email appeared, people began to step into a real two-way networks era. After that, BBS came into the world, made the ¡°mass mailer¡± and information sharing come true; instant messaging came into the world, made online chatting come true; blog came into the world, made an updated BBS with better parallel processing capability come true. Step by step, the rudiment of the social networks today becomes clear and clear.

As we all know, the most important feature of social networks is interactive. The key technology that supports interaction is Web 2.0 modality. Web 2.0 allows users to participate in providing information for the page, instead of server does it itself. Web 2.0 emphasizes interaction, including interaction between server and users, users and users, and website and other websites. Core of Web 2.0 is not a technology, but an ideology. There is no distinct boundary between Web 1.0 and Web 2.0, so social networks just come into the world step by step, not by an exact time of certain technology appears.
