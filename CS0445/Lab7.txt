		A QUIZ on REGULAR EXPRESSIONS

Your expression must reject any string not containing the described pattern.
Your expression must match  any string containing the described pattern.

1) .*([01]\\d|2[0-3]):[0-5]\\d:[0-5]\\d.*
              
2) ([a-zA-Z]+([0-9]|)){0,8}

3) .*[0-9]{4}/(0[0-9]|1[0-2])/([012][0-9]|3[01]).*

4) ^CS-0[0-9]{3}$

5) (.*\\d{5}(-\\d{4})?|.*\\d{5}[^-].*)
